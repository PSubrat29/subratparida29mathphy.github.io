About Me

👤

Mathematical Physicist focused on the geometric validity of gravity, topological nature of dimensionality, and the mathematical architecture of the universe.

M.Sc. (Honours) in Mathematics from Pondicherry University (2024) with research in Mathematical General Relativity and Theory of Gravity. My work explores the underlying constraints of reference frames, the fundamental structure of time, and the precise definitions of freedom within physical systems through advanced formalism.
